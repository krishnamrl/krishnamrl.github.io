Krishna Murali

I'm a flight controls engineer, currently at Ascendance, where I work on the end-to-end design, simulation, and testing of an electric vertical take-off and landing (eVTOL) aircraft.

Previously, as a research assistant at IONLAB @ ISAE-SUPAERO, I developed guidance and control architectures for a highly dynamic tail-sitter drone. I also spent time building and field-testing controllers at Dronisos for drone light shows at Disneyland, and competing in the Dassault UAV Challenge, where I dipped my toes into embedded software.

I like the idea of leveraging a vehicle's natural physics to ensure its safe and reliable operation.
